Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Location:Great Nicobar Island

Formed: 1992

Features: Part of Great Nicobar Biosphere Reserve

Flora & Fauna:Crab-eating macaque, Nicobar treeshrew

Location:Great, Pulobaha, Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Formed: 1992

Features: Galathea River

Flora & Fauna: Crab-eating macaques, giant leatherback turtles, Nicobar tree shrews, saltwater crocodiles, monitor lizards, palm civets, Malayan box turtles and pythons.

Andhra Pradesh

Location:Near Rajamahendravaram

Formed: 2008

Features: Important bird and biodiversity area by BirdLife International

Flora & Fauna:Royal Bengal Tiger, Leopards, rusty-spotted cat, King cobra

Location:Near Tirumala, Seshachalam Hills and Tirumala Hills

Formed: 1989

Features: Godavari River

Flora & Fauna: Yellow-throated bulbul, White-rumped vulture

Arunachal Pradesh

Location:Siang Valley

Formed: 1986

Features: biodiversity in the natural forests

Flora & Fauna:takin, goral, Indian leopard, Bengal tiger, barking deer, serow and red panda

Location:Near Miao

Formed: 1983

Features: part of Mizoram Manipur Kachin rain forests

Flora & Fauna: Sapria himalayana, Namdapha flying squirrel, red panda, dhole, Malayan sun bear, Indian wolf, Asiatic black bear, gaur, stump-tailed macaque, Assamese macaque, rhesus macaque.

Assam

Location:Near Tinsukia

Formed: 1999

Features: Largest Swamp Forest in North-east India

Flora & Fauna: Feral horse, Wild water buffalo, Ganges river dolphin etc

Location:Near Gossaigaon

Formed: 2021

Features: Part of Eastern Himalayan Foothills

Flora & Fauna: Gee's golden langur

Bihar

Location:Near Valmiki Nagar, Bagaha

Formed: 1978

Features: Home of Tharu people in India

Flora & Fauna:Bengal tiger, Indian gaur, Indian rhinoceros, Asian black bear, Indian elephant, Indian leopard

Chhattisgarh

Location:Near Ambikapur, Manendragarh, Chirimiri

Formed: 1981

Features: Largest national park of Chhattisgarh

Flora & Fauna: mammals like tigers, leopards, nilgai, jackal, antelope, wild boar, bison, hyena porcupine, and various other species

Location:Near Dantewada

Formed: 1982

Flora & Fauna: Wild Buffaloes, Tigers, Hill Mynas

Goa

Location:Near Molem

Formed: 1978

Features: Famous Dudhsagar Falls located inside also a Mahadev Temple of Kadamba architecture is also located inside

Flora & Fauna:Deer, mouse deer, hog, and barking deer are all herbivores that live in the sanctuary

Gujarat

Location:Near Bhavnagar, Vallabhipur, Dholera, Dhandhuka

Formed: 1976

Features: A grassland ecosystem for conservation of Blackbuck, Lesser florican

Flora & Fauna:Blackbucks, Indian grey wolf, striped hyena, Bengal fox, golden jackal, jungle cat and many other small mammals

Location:Near Sasangir, Tulsishyam, Talala, Gir Gadhada

Formed: 1965

Features: Only habitat of Asiatic Lion

Flora & Fauna:Asiatic lion, Indian Leopard, Stripped Hyna, Golden Jackle

Haryana

Location:Near Bilaspur(Yamunanagar district), Paonta Sahib

Formed: 2003

Features: Located in Sivalik Hills

Flora & Fauna: Leopard, Ghoral, Barking deer, Sambar, Chital, Python, King Cobra, Monitor lizard etc

Location:Near Gurugram

Formed: 1991

Features: Declared as Ramsar Wetland in 2021

Flora & Fauna:many birds, animals such as blue bull, Indian Fox and black buck

Himachal Pradesh

Location:Near Kullu

Formed: 1985

Features: Declared as UNESCO World Heritage Site in 2014 popular trekking and ecotourism destination

Flora & Fauna:Bharal, snow leopard, Himalayan brown bear, Himalayan tahr, Musk deer

Location:Near Kasol

Formed: 2010

Features: Popular destination for Hiking, Canyoning

Flora & Fauna:Snow leopard, blue sheep, Himalayan brown bear, Himalayan tahr, musk deer, and Goral

Jammu and Kashmir

Location:Near Srinagar

Formed: 1981

Features: Only area where Hangul is found

Flora & Fauna:Hangul, Thar, Leopard cat, Musk deer, Snow Leopard, Himalayan black bear, Himalayan brown bear, Otter

Location:Near Baramulla

Formed: 1992

Features: Located on Line of Control

Flora & Fauna:Markhor, Himalayan musk deer, Golden eagle, Snow pigeon

Jharkhand

Location:Near Medininagar

Formed: 1974

Flora & Fauna:Tiger, Indian bison, elephant, hyenas, monkey, leopard

Karnataka

Location:Kodthalli

Formed: 1987

Features: Part of Kali Tiger Reserve

Flora & Fauna:Great hornbill, tiger, leopard, black panther, bear, elephant.

Location:Chamarajnagar

Formed: 1974

Features: Dry deciduous forest is dominant. Also include moist deciduous forests and shrublands.

Flora & Fauna:Chital, Bengal tiger, gray langurs, Indian giant squirrel, gaur, leopard, sambar deer, Indian elephants, honey buzzard, red-headed vulture

West Bengal

Location: Near Kolkata

Formed: 1987

Features: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Tiger Reserve

Flora & Fauna: Bengal Tiger, Saltwater Crocodile, Mangrove Forests

Location: Jalpaiguri

Formed: 1994

Features: Known for Indian rhinoceros population

Flora & Fauna: Indian rhinoceros, Asian elephant, Bengal tiger, diverse bird species

Location: Alipurduar district

Formed: 1941

Features:Indian one-horned rhinoceros

Flora & Fauna: Indian one-horned rhinoceros,Indian leopard, Indian elephant Mangrove Forests

Uttarakhand

Location: Ramnagar, Nainital

Formed: 1936 (India's first national park)

Features: Known for Bengal Tigers and Dhikala Grasslands

Flora & Fauna: Bengal Tiger, Asian Elephant, Diverse Bird Species

Location: Chamoli

Formed: 1982

Features: UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its high-altitude flora

Flora & Fauna: Snow Leopard, Himalayan Musk Deer, Flora unique to the Himalayan ecosystem

Madhya Pradesh

Location: Mandla and Balaghat districts

Formed: 1955

Features: Known for Barasingha (Swamp Deer) and lush sal forests

Flora & Fauna: Bengal Tiger, Indian Leopard, Barasingha, diverse bird species

Location: Umaria district

Formed: 1968

Features: Highest known density of Bengal Tigers

Flora & Fauna: Bengal Tiger, Indian Leopard, Sambar Deer, various bird species
